# SCENE ARCHITECTURE

The website is structured as a cinematic sequence.

---

Scene 1 — Signal

Introduce the problem.

The internet is noisy.

Products fail because they never reach the right networks.

---

Scene 2 — Systems

Introduce engineering capability.

Focus:

software systems  
AI tools  
interactive web technology

---

Scene 3 — Networks

Introduce ecosystem building.

Focus:

partnerships  
communities  
global connections

---

Scene 4 — Distribution

Introduce growth systems.

Focus:

KOL networks  
content propagation  
viral loops

---

Scene 5 — The Machine

Combine systems + networks + distribution.

Show the agency as a launch engine.

---

Scene 6 — Deployments

Show real projects.

---

Scene 7 — Launch

Call to action.

Invite collaboration.
